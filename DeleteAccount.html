Account Deletion Request for RideSync

If you would like to delete your RideSync account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below. This will permanently remove your account information, including your travel history, event creations, and any personal data stored in the app.

Steps to Request Account Deletion

  1. Click on the "Delete My Account" link below.
  2. Send an email to ridesync2024@gmail.com with your account details to request deletion.
  3. Our team will process your request within 7 business days.
Delete My Account

Data Deletion Policy

Once your account is deleted, all data related to your account, including trip plans, events, and group chat history, will be permanently removed from our servers. This action is irreversible, and you will not be able to recover any deleted information.

If you have any questions, please contact us at ridesync2024@gmail.com.
